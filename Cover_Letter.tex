\documentclass{class/cover_letter}

%----------------------------------------------------------
% 基本宏包配置
%----------------------------------------------------------
\usepackage[english]{babel}

%----------------------------------------------------------
% 主题色配置 (可选: red, purple, blue)
%----------------------------------------------------------
\rhotheme{red}

%----------------------------------------------------------
% 论文信息（定义一次，全局复用）
% - Subject 行自动由 \papertitle + \journalname 生成
% - 正文中用 \thepapertitle 引用带引号的标题
% - 正文中用 \thejournalname 引用带斜体的期刊名
%----------------------------------------------------------
\papertitle{Your Paper Title Goes Here}
\journalname{IEEE Transactions on Mobile Computing}

%----------------------------------------------------------
% 机构 Logo（可选，显示在首页页眉左侧）
%----------------------------------------------------------

%----------------------------------------------------------
% 发件人信息 (Sender)
%----------------------------------------------------------
\sendername{Prof. Xiaolin Jia}
\senderaffiliation{Department of Computer Science \\ University Name}
\senderaddress{Xi'an, Shaanxi, China, 710000}
\senderemail{my\_jiaxl@163.com}
% \senderphone{+86-xxx-xxxx-xxxx}    % 可选

%----------------------------------------------------------
% 收件人信息 (Recipient)
%----------------------------------------------------------
\recipientname{Dr. Editor Name}
\recipienttitle{Editor-in-Chief}
\recipientaddress{Editorial Office}

%----------------------------------------------------------
% 称呼与结束语
%----------------------------------------------------------
\opening{Dear Dr. Editor,}
\closing{Sincerely,}

%----------------------------------------------------------
% 正文
%----------------------------------------------------------
\begin{document}

\makeheader

%% ===== 正文部分 =====
%% 在此撰写投稿信内容
%% 可直接使用 \thepapertitle 和 \thejournalname 引用论文/期刊名

I am writing to submit our manuscript entitled \thepapertitle\ for consideration of publication in \thejournalname.

This paper proposes a novel protocol for RFID missing tag identification that addresses three critical challenges: link reliability, time-slot waste, and channel utilization. Our main contributions include:

\begin{itemize}
    \item A link-adaptive mechanism for robust communication under varying channel conditions
    \item An orthogonal coding scheme that enables concurrent tag responses
    \item A de-slotted protocol design that eliminates empty slot overhead
\end{itemize}

Extensive simulations demonstrate that the proposed protocol significantly outperforms existing methods in both identification speed and reliability.

We confirm that this manuscript has not been published elsewhere and is not under consideration by another journal. All authors have approved the manuscript and agree with its submission to \thejournalname.

We look forward to your favorable response.

\makeclosing

\end{document}
